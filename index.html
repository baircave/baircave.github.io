Our Ethos

At Odd Harmonics, sound is a sandbox.

We cultivate musicianship by first prioritizing hands-on creative experimentation. No expertise required. Once we've developed an understanding of the building blocks of sound, that's when we dive into more traditional musical concepts.

Our intention is not to compete with conservatory-style teachings but rather complement them. By focusing on creating sonic worlds, we aim to unlock an underrated and highly accessible axis of expression.

About Our Founder

Nick Newhouse began his 10+ year career in music by composing for ads at The Music Playground in Lower Manhattan. He's since released music with independent record labels such as Tiny Waves, Mad Zoo, New Dawn Collective, & Materia Music, and has DJed events around the country.

His music background started with drum, guitar, and piano lessons early in life, but his love for music making began in earnest at Brown University where he majored in both Computer Music and Computer Science.
